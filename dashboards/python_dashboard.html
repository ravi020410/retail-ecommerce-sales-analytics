Core Business Problem

Direct-to-consumer e-commerce brands often struggle to accurately monitor cart abandonment rates and identify which acquisition channels yield the highest customer lifetime value (LTV). Lacking programmatic ETL pipelines and detailed database segmentation, teams deploy broad marketing incentives that result in profit margin erosion.

This repository addresses this limitation by developing an end-to-end **E-Commerce Funnel & Revenue Intelligence Pipeline**. By joining web analytics session activity logs with transactional order databases, we calculate full-funnel conversion rates, promo code sensitivities, device-type performance, and customer lifetime value deciles.

Immediate Funnel Interventions

  • Cart Abandonment Email Retargeting: E-Commerce funnel metrics highlight over **$1,200,000 in monthly potential revenues** left uncompleted in shopping carts. Set up active browser exit-intent popups and launch automated, 3-tier cart abandonment emails (1 hour, 12 hours, 24 hours post-abandonment) offering free shipping to capture high-intent buyers.
  • Mobile Checkout Optimization: Mobile users exhibit a low checkout initiation rate (24% vs. 38% for desktop) due to slow form-fields and unoptimized coupon code entry layouts. Restructure mobile checkouts to include 1-click Express Pay (Google/Apple Pay) option to increase mobile conversions.
